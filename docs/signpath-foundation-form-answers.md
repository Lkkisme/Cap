## Required Consent

The owner must personally confirm:

- `I have read and agree to the SignPath Foundation Code of Conduct and I understand that certificates are issued in SignPath Foundation's name and may be revoked if terms are violated.`
- `I agree to allow SignPath to store and process my personal data.`

The optional marketing consent can stay unchecked.
